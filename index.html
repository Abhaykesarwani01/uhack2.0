F.A.Q

  • What is the eligibility criteria and cost for participation?

    You must assemble a team comprising minimum 1 and maximum 6 members, and all team members should be current students at any college located in Prayagraj, regardless of their branch or year of study. We encourage participants from various academic backgrounds, but we do expect all team members to demonstrate problem-solving abilities and the capability to craft innovative solutions.Cost of paticipation is Rs.120/ per team.

  • what will be the location and timing for grand finale.

    Our hackathon's grand finale will be held at UCER Campus, Prayagraj, on October 13th and 14th. The event will run from 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM on both days. Finalist will receive details and the event schedule closer to the date via emails, social media channels.

  • What should I bring to the event?

    You need to carry your college ID Card, laptop, phone, chargers and a geeky t-shirt!
