GUIDELINES AND PROCESS FLOW FOR U-HACK 2.0

How do I register for UHack 2.0

Students can directly register themselves by registration desk at your college . Or you can register yourself by clicking on https://uhack2.netlify.app/

TEAM FORMATION

1. All team members of UGI and other colleges from Prayagraj are allowed.

2. Each team would contains atmost 6 members and minimum 3 members including the team leader

3. As the software edition of the hackathon is digital product development competition, majority of the team members MUST be well versed with programming skills. IDEA SELECTION CRITERIA

Post Idea submission process, the ideas will be evaluated by experts. Evaluation criteria will include novelty of the idea, complexity, clarity and details in the prescribed format, feasibility, practicability, sustainability, scale of impact, user experience and potential for future work progression.

MODE OF HACKATHON

U-Hack 2022 will be held offline at United College of Engineering and Research and teams have to present at respective centre . The dates for the hackathon is 4th/14th of September 2023

Judging Criteria

Projects will be judged based on creativity, technical complexity, and impact.

SHORTLISTED IDEAS ANNOUNCEMENT

After stage of pre-screening of submitted ideas notification about selected teams will be put up on our portal and will also be sent to their respective email IDs

Be available for meetings, sessions and trainings during the preparation phase; you will be notified about them in advance.
